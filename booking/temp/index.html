How the people like to play

Soccer is popular globally largely because it was popular amongst the British merchants and sailors who traded and spread their culture around the world in the late 19th century. The sport evolved in the British public (private) school system.

and when those school boys grew up and traveled the world they brought their academic culture with them, of which an integral part was physical fitness through team sports. Because the British trade empire was so enormous during that time, and because the sport was so popular amongst the sailors who composed that empire, just about every place on Earth was exposed to the sport of football between 1875 and 1925
